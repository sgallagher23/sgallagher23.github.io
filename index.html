About Me

Sean Gallagher is a Chicago-based Consulting Summer Analyst in Accenture's Consulting Development Program. During his Summer with Accenture, he has developed an interest in Applied Research & Development Services which assists pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies globally to leverage the capabilities of tomorrow. Because of his background in Neuroscience and understanding of trends within the Life Sciences industry, he is determined to enable pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies to improve patient outcomes by integrating scientific expertise with the patient experience.

Sean is a rising senior at Vanderbilt University studying Neuroscience and Philosophy. He has experience as a Research Assistant in Vanderbilt's Stress and Early Adversity Lab which studies early life development through fMRI analysis of the adolescent brain.

He is also experienced with Dental Sleep Medicine. As an intern at the American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine (AADSM), he facilitated providing educational opportunities to both dentists and patients about the role of Oral Appliance Therapy in treating Obstructive Sleep Apnea. During his internship at the AADSM, he spearheaded the process of obtaining a Google Ad Grant in the amount of $10,000 per month to further Dental Sleep Medicine education through the use of Google Ads and Google Analytics. Over the course of three months, he also bolstered the social media presence of the AADSM by increasing tweets by 533.33%, Twitter impressions by 68.9%, profile visits by 255.1%, and Twitter mentions by 120%.

At Vanderbilt, Sean has served in a variety of leadership roles. He is the current President of the Pre-Dental Society, Pre-Dental Representatative on the Health Professions Advisory Office Student Leadership Board, Internal Secretary of University Catholic, and Photography Chair of Beta Upsilon Chi. He is also an active volunteer with Preston Taylor Ministries and ACEing Autism.
